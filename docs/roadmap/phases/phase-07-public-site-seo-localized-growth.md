Bring the public growth surface to production quality: homepage, pricing, language pages, niche pages, voice pages, and localized SEO structure.

## Why This Phase Comes Here

Public growth surfaces should reflect real product capability, not promises that are still ahead of implementation.

## Tracks Involved

- Workflow

1. Workflow, quality, billing, and notifications are already grounded.
2. Content truth and entitlement truth are stable enough for public copy.

2. Locale routing and language pages
3. Niche pages and voice pages

2. Public pricing copy must stay tied to entitlement truth.

## Exit Criteria

1. Public pages and product reality agree.
2. Localized growth surfaces are powered by the content truth system rather than hand-maintained page copies.

## Deliverables

1. Production-grade public site
2. Localized SEO surface
3. Public growth pages aligned with true plan capabilities
