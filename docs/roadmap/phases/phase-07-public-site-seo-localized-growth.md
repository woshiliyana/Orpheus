## Goal

Expand the public growth surface after the first funnel foundation exists: multilingual SEO pages, broader public growth pages, and localized expansion around the stable English product core.

## Why This Phase Comes Here

Public growth expansion should reflect real product capability, not become the first place where distribution is finally designed.

## Tracks Involved

- Experience
- Workflow

## Entry Criteria

1. Workflow, quality, billing, and notifications are already grounded.
2. Content truth and entitlement truth are stable enough for public copy.

## In-Phase Focus

1. Multilingual SEO landing-page expansion
2. Additional niche pages and voice pages
3. Structured metadata, robots, sitemap, hreflang
4. Localized content operations through the content truth system
5. Growth-surface expansion beyond the first funnel foundation

## Parallel Guidance

1. Locale expansion and page component work can fan out once content truth is stable.
2. Public pricing copy must stay tied to entitlement truth.

## Exit Criteria

1. Public pages and product reality agree.
2. Localized growth surfaces are powered by the content truth system rather than hand-maintained page copies.

## Deliverables

1. Production-grade public site
2. Localized SEO surface
3. Public growth pages aligned with true plan capabilities
